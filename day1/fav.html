Legend of Hanuman

About the Series

"Legend of Hanuman" is an animated series that tells the epic story of Lord Hanuman from the Ramayana. It is a thrilling and visually captivating journey that delves into the lesser-known details of Hanuman's life.

Series Details

Plot

The series follows the adventures of Lord Hanuman, his extraordinary powers, and his unwavering devotion to Lord Rama. It explores his role in the rescue of Sita, the battle with Ravana, and various other legendary feats.
